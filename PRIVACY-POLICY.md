# Privacy Policy for Wati Comfort - Sender Tag

**Last Updated**: January 30, 2026

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Wati Comfort - Sender Tag ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect:
- Your email address
- Your name
- Your Google profile picture URL

This information is collected solely for the purpose of identifying you as a user within your WATI workspace.

### Usage Data
We store the following information in our backend database:
- Phone numbers of customers you interact with on WATI
- Last replier information
- Default sales assignments
- Chat status
- Message timestamps

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
1. **Identify users**: Display your name as a sender tag on messages in WATI
2. **Track assignments**: Remember which sales representative is assigned to which customer
3. **Improve workflow**: Provide default sales assignments and status tracking
4. **Enable multi-user collaboration**: Allow multiple users to work on a single WATI account

## Data Storage and Security

- User data is stored securely on Cloudflare Workers and Cloudflare D1 Database
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- Your Google OAuth tokens are handled securely by Chrome's identity API
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties

## Data Retention

- User profile information is retained as long as you use the extension
- Chat and customer data is retained indefinitely to maintain historical records
- You can request deletion of your data by contacting us (see below)

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses:
- **Google OAuth**: For user authentication (subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Cloudflare**: For backend hosting and database (subject to [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing
